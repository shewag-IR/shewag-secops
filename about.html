About the Platform

Built for Defenders.
By a Defender.

shewag-secops is a solo-built, free cybersecurity training platform focused entirely on the Blue Team — SOC analysts, incident responders, and threat hunters who protect organisations from real attacks.

The Story

Why This Exists

When I started in cybersecurity, I quickly found that most free training was either heavily offensive-focused, too theoretical, or hidden behind expensive subscription walls. Blue Team skills — the skills defenders actually need every day — were underserved.

shewag-secops is my answer to that gap. Every lab here is based on real attack patterns, real tooling, and real methodology that SOC analysts use in production environments. No padding, no fluff, no paywalls.

Labs are designed to be completed in under 2 hours, run entirely offline on your own machine, and leave you with something concrete: a detection rule, an incident report, or a new skill you can apply on the job tomorrow.
